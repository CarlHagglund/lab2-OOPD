MVC implementation thoughts:
1. First implementation of MVC given to us was sloppy and did not line up with the ideal design of a MVC implementation.
2. The whole point of separating MVC is to improve the codes organization, maintainability and debugging.
Each class handles specific responsibility's.


Design patterns:
Observer pattern:
tightly coupled with subjects holding direct references to its observer.
In this case it is CarLogic with CarController, where CarController holds direct references.

Factory Method: We have made a create() and destory() method which is used in the GUI, create() creates random cars,
destroy() removes the last Car in the list.
It would increase scalability, extendability
and usability.

State: We are not using a state design pattern,
this would change the entire design of our car classes and make a refactor crazy.

Composite: Composite is used as the structure for the lab, this was intentional as we wanted Car as a "godclass".
This means that all the subclasses implements Car and therefor creates a tree structure. Although the subclasses
can still be worked with as individual objects
